About me

My name is Giuseppe Lenti, I am 29 years old and I am a UX/UI Designer with a background in graphic design and a passion for designing intuitive, accessible and user-centered digital experiences. I work mainly with tools such as Figma, Illustrator for graphic design, while for the development phase I rely on VSCODE for designing in HTML and CSS, to create responsive interfaces that combine aesthetics and functionality. After a diploma in Advertising Graphics, I continued my training in Web Design in Naples and in UX/UI Design with online courses. I gained experience as a Software Tester for DaVinci Engineering between Germany and Italy, contributing to the opening of a new testing office in Italy thanks to the effectiveness of our work. I have also worked in the commercial sector and as a private driver, developing excellent interpersonal and problem-solving skills. In my free time, I am inspired by films, books, music and video games, elements that fuel my creativity. I firmly believe in the importance of continuous learning and in the value of a positive work environment, where you grow by sharing skills and experiences.
